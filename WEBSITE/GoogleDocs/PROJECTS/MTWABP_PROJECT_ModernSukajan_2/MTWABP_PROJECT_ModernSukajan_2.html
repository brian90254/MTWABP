Modern Sukajan

 

OVERVIEW:

This is a contemporary custom jacket made in the style of a Sukajan (or “souvenir”) jacket. As in the traditional Japanese version, it’s made of satin, patterned like a baseball jacket, and fully reversible.

  •  Artwork for this jacket comes from the Tattoo Mystique homebrew pinball machine.
  •  One side utilizes pre-embroidered patches, while the second side uses heat-transfer prints.
  •  Quilting with fiberfill or silk batting is optional, and usually attached to just one side.
  •  Standard rib knit is used for the waist, cuffs, and collar.
  •  Reversible metal zipper.
