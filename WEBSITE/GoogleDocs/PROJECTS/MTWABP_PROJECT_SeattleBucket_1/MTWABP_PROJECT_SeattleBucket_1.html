The ORIGINAL Seattle Bucket Hat

OVERVIEW:

This custom design is a distinctly Seattle take on the traditional “bucket hat”, with features suited for the unique climate:

  •  Tapered crown is more like the profile of a Panama hat, so less likely for water to pool on top.
  •  The brim is wider, to help shed rain farther away from the body.
  •  The outside is made of “softshell” material, which is a thin stackup of spandex, TPU, and fleece, making it water repellent.
  •  There’s a sherpa liner for warmth in the rain or cold.
  •  The hat is also reversible, making it cool in the summertime.
