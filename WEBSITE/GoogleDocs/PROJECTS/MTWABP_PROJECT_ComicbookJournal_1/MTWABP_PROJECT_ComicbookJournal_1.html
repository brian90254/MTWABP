Comicbook Journal

 

OVERVIEW:

This project up-cycles worn out comic book covers into blank sketchbook journals. The how-to videos walk through the process to prep, laminate, trim, and punch the covers, as well as cutting and punching the blank paper if needed.  

  •  Repurpose comic books that would otherwise be thrown out, capturing some fantastic artwork and the nostalgia that goes with these childhood classics.
  •  Rectangular hole punch is a 2:1, meaning the larger 2-holes-per-inch size.
  •  Binding is Wire-O, used in many commercially available sketchbooks and journals.
  •  Paper can also be cut to size, giving you flexibility to use your preferred texture and weight media.
